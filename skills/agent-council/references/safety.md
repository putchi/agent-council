# Safety

## General

- Do not share API keys, passwords, or credentials as part of a council question.
- Do not include personally identifiable information (PII) about third parties.
- All questions are sent to Claude via the local `claude` CLI — treat them as any cloud-processed request.

## Career and Personal councils

- Advice from the council is AI-generated and does not replace professional guidance
  (lawyers, therapists, financial advisors, doctors).
- For high-stakes personal or legal decisions, use the council for framing and perspective,
  then verify with a qualified professional.

## Business council

- Do not include confidential business data, unreleased financials, or trade secrets
  in council questions unless you are comfortable with that data being processed by
  Claude under your existing Claude Code terms of service.
